Covers, Chapters and Topics

Definitions

Name

Description

Cover

The cover page of your document.

Chapter

A chapter in your document. It can have its own cover page.

Table of contents

An automatic Table of contents that is generated when you export your document.

Topic

Content to add to your document. Not necessary a single page. Written in Markdown.

Table of contents

You can change the title of the table of contents by renaming the item in the left panel.

For the moment it is not possible to customize the table of contents look.
